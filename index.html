Teaching Writing

In the Age of AI

In late 2025, we interviewed 7 colleagues at the University of Mississippi about how Generative AI has influenced the way they teach writing. We chose these interview subjects because they represent a wide range of experiences and perspectives, from full professor to graduate teaching assistant. Each subject was provided with the general context in advance of the interview, but as you will hear below, the conversations were free-flowing. We hope these interviews will inform you, your colleagues, and your students.
